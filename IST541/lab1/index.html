How to Sanitize Wrestling Mats using a Plastic Industrial Sprayer

By Oscar Mendez

Step 1: Assure you have all of the tools necessary for disinfecting the wrestling mat

When it comes to a close-contact sport such as wrestling, disinfecting the wrestling mat is an essential requirement in preventing skin lesions. The items below will be needed to prepare the disinfectant spray:

  • Industrial Sprayer
  • 2 Gallons of water(Storage Closet)
  • 2 oz of Disinfectant Solution
  • 2 oz Measuring Cup

The Industrial Sprayer should have the following parts:

  • 12-inch straight wand
  • 28-inch reinforced hose
  • 2-gallon container
Step 2: Locate the water source in the Storage Closet

If the storage closet is locked, it can be opened by a custodian or the instructor on-site. After assuring that you have access to the storage closet, look for the the 3-way water hose valve and the corresponding black hose that are behind the washer and dryer machine.

Step 3: Fill the 2-gallon Container with Water

Place the black hose in the container and turn the black valve upwards to allow the water to flow. Fill the 2-gallon container with water up to the 2-gallon marker written on the side of the translucent container. Do not fill the industrial sprayer past the 2-gallon marker to prevent future overflow when addding the disinfectant solution. Turn the black valve downwards to stop water flow.

Step 4: Add the disinfectant cleaning solution

Fill the sprayer with 1 ounce of the disinfectant cleaning solution. This is to assure that each ounce of the cleaning solution is diluted with a gallon of water.

  • MatPRO EX Pour Concentrated Formula (contains Ammonium Chloride)
  • Turf Guard Surface Disinfectant (contains Isopropyl Alcohol 70%)
Step 5: Seal the 2-gallon container

Before inserting the sprayer, press the top handle of the pump all the way down and twist it clockwise to lock it in place. Turn the whole pump clockwise into the 2-gallon container until it is fully sealed.

Step 6: Pump the Industrial Sprayer

Unlock the pump handle by turning the top handle counter-clockwise while keeping the bottom of the pump intact.

Pump the industrial sprayer 2-3 times to create internal air pressure.

Step 7: Adjust the straight-wand to dispense the proper amount of spray

The tip of the straight-wand is adjustable to assure the appropriate amount of spray is being dispensed. The dispensed spray must cover the most amount of surface area.If the spray dispenses in a singular stream, loosen up the tip of the spray wand.
